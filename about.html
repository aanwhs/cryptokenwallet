About - CrypTokenWallet

CrypTokenWallet is an Interface




CrypTokenWallet is a free, client-side interface helping you interact with the blockchain technology. The easy-to-use, open-source platform allows you to generate wallets, interact with smart contracts, and so much more.

CrypTokenWallet does not hold your keys for you. CrypTokenWallet cannot access accounts, recover keys, reset passwords, nor reverse transactions. Protect your keys & always check that you are on correct URL. You and only you are responsible for your security.




  • When you create an account on CrypTokenWallet you are generating a cryptographic set of numbers: your private key and your public key (address).
  • The handling of your keys happens entirely on your computer, inside your browser.
  • CrypTokenWallet never transmit, receive or store your private key, password, or other account information.
  • CrypTokenWallet do not charge a transaction fee.
  • You are simply using CrypTokenWallet interface to interact directly with the blockchain.
