Cookie Policy

Last updated: May 26, 2023




CrypTokenWallet Websites may use cookies to distinguish you from other users of CrypTokenWallet Websites. This may help CrypTokenWallet to provide you with a good experience when you browse CrypTokenWallet Websites and may also allow CrypTokenWallet to improve CrypTokenWallet Websites. By continuing to browse the Websites, you are agreeing to CrypTokenWallet use of cookies as well as the terms of this policy (the “Cookie Policy”). A cookie is a small file of letters and numbers that CrypTokenWallet may store on your browser or the hard drive of your computer if you agree. Cookies contain information that is transferred to your computer's hard drive.

You can find more information about the individual cookies CrypTokenWallet may use and the purposes for which CrypTokenWallet may use them in the table below:

# Cookie Name Purpose
1ConnectToWalletUsingUsed to know what you are using in the CrypTokenWallet interface to connect to the blockchain.
2MnemonicUsed to connect your wallet with the blockchain using mnemonics.
3PasswordUsed to connect your wallet with the blockchain using a mnemonic and password.
4WalletPrivateKeyUsed to connect your wallet with the blockchain using a private key.
5NodeURLUsed to connect your wallet to the blockchain.

You can block cookies by activating the setting on your browser that allows you to refuse the setting of all or some cookies. However, if you use your browser settings to block all cookies (including essential cookies), you may not be able to access all or parts of CrypTokenWallet sites.
